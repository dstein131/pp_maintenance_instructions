Payment Plan Maintenance

Select Property

Fill in the appropriate property or sale ID into the Property Selection dialog box and click on the ‘Submit’ button. NOTE: When you begin typing, the system will start to display potential matches to the ID you are typing.

Payment Plan Details

If the property ID entered has an active payment plan, you will see details of this plan along with the general ability to maintain the plan. Tasks like record a payment, update the plan status, view/download the agreement and view pay history area available in this area.

Recording a Payment

To record a payment by the property owner, enter the date of the payment followed by the amount and select ‘Save Payment’. NOTE: You’ll notice the down payment of $100 is also listed here. We used the down payment field when the property owner wanted to make a payment on the same day as the payment plan was created.

NOTE: The payment of $508 is recorded in the payment history. Additional payments recorded will fall below the previous payment. All payments will be shown under the Property Maintenance menu item as a partial payment.

Updating Plan Status

Updating the plan status will allow you to change the overall status of the payment plan using the available options in the pull-down menu. NOTE: By completing this process, it does not update the status within the system for the parcel itself. Please refer to the section titled Updating Status within Tax Sale System if the county chooses to update the status once a payment plan is activated.

  • ACTIVE: Payment plan has been activated and is current.
  • ACTIVE – UNVERIFIED: Payment plan has been activated but email / SMS hasn’t been verified and is current.
  • ACTIVE – VERIFIED: Payment plan has been activated and email / SMS has been verified and is current.
  • CLOSED: Payment plan has been satisfied.
  • CLOSED DELINQUENT: Payment plan has not been satisfied due to non-payments.
  • DELINQUENT: Payment plan has been activated but payments are not current.
  • PENDING: Payment plan has been set up but not activated. **Default payment plan status**
